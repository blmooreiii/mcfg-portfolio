1. Introduction

Marble Ceilings LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates Ultra Black — The Blackest Trivia Game You Know ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

This Privacy Policy should be read alongside our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use the App, you may provide:

  • Account information: Display name, email address, and password
  • Profile preferences: Avatar selection, preferred deck categories
  • Payment information: We do not store payment card details directly. All payment processing is handled by Apple Inc. (App Store) or Google LLC (Google Play). We receive only transaction confirmation and subscription status from these platforms.
  • Communications: If you contact us for support, we collect the content of your messages and any information you voluntarily provide

2b. Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Games played, questions answered, scores, badge achievements, session duration, deck selections, answer choices, and in-app navigation patterns
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and App version
  • Performance data: App crashes, error logs, and diagnostic information
  • Session data: Login timestamps, session length, and frequency of use

2c. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Apple / Google: Subscription status, transaction IDs, and account identifiers when you log in through Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In
  • Analytics providers: Aggregated and anonymized usage data from analytics services we use to improve the App
  • Advertising networks: If you use the free tier, our advertising partners may provide us with information about ad interactions subject to their own privacy policies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Operate the App

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and track subscription status and entitlements
  • Deliver gameplay, questions, badges, and scores
  • Save your progress, streaks, and achievement history
  • Enforce daily game limits for free tier users

Improve Our Services

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve question content, game mechanics, and app performance
  • Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features and content decks
  • Conduct internal research and analytics

Communicate with You

  • Send transactional notifications (subscription renewals, account updates)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send optional promotional communications if you opt in (you may opt out at any time)

Serve Advertising & Legal

  • Display third-party advertisements between game rounds for free tier users
  • Share limited data with advertising partners to serve relevant ads, subject to your device privacy settings
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of Marble Ceilings LLC, our users, and the public

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

4a. Service Providers

We share information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Analytics: PostHog or similar analytics platforms to understand App usage (data is minimized and anonymized where possible)
  • Error tracking: Sentry or similar services to monitor App stability
  • Cloud infrastructure: Supabase or similar database and backend providers who store App data on our behalf
  • Customer support: Help desk tools if you contact us for support

All service providers are contractually required to use your information only to provide services to us and to maintain appropriate security standards.

4b. Advertising Partners (Free Tier Only)

If you use the free tier, we work with third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob or Meta Audience Network) to display ads within the App. These partners may collect and use data about you subject to their own privacy policies. You can limit ad tracking through your device settings:

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → opt out of personalized ads
4c. Platform Partners

We share data with Apple and Google as necessary to process subscriptions and in-app purchases, authenticate accounts, and comply with App Store and Google Play requirements.

4d. Business Transfers

If Marble Ceilings LLC is acquired by or merges with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4e. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, subpoena, court order, or other governmental request, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or comply with a legal obligation.

4f. With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the App. Specifically:

Account Data Life of account + 90 days after deletion
Game Session Data 2 years (retained for achievement history & leaderboards)
Payment Records 7 years (as required by tax & financial regulations)
Support Contacts 2 years (for messages & inquiries)

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive account data
  • Access controls limiting who within Marble Ceilings LLC can access your data
  • Regular security assessments of our infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

Important Notice: Ultra Black is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address in Section 13.
